3D Medical Image Registration & Segmentation
=============================================
.. important::
   This tutorial demonstrates fundamental concepts of 3D medical image processing:
   registration (aligning images) and segmentation (identifying anatomical regions),
   using self-contained synthetic data with no heavy dependencies.

  - **NAIC Portal**: https://www.naic.no/
  - **Orchestrator**: https://orchestrator.naic.no/
  - **Repository**: https://github.com/NAICNO/wp7-UC4-medical-image-registration
